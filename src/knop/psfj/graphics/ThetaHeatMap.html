Theta heatmap
The color coding of the theta heatmap is based on two variables : the angle and the asymmetry. For each angle, there is a particular color. When the asymmetry is close to 1 (which means the bead is roughly round), this color fades to white, whereas for values significantly smaller than 1 (and increasing asymmetry), the colors are more intense.
