Heat Map

Heat maps are generated using the median of a sliding window along the x and y axes. The window size and the step size for window movements are calculated based on bead density. Heat maps are pseudo colored and scaled. The plots to the right represent projections of the parameters along the indicated axes. For scaling, two alternative options are possible and can be chosen from above: normalized scaling and absolute scaling (in µm).

Statistics

Averaging window size (= 1px) : %windowSize
Mean # of beads per pixels : %mean
